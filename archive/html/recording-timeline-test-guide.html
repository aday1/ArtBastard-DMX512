üêõ Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions:

Issue: No events being recorded

Solution: Ensure recording is active (red Record button highlighted) and make significant slider movements (‚â•1 DMX unit).

Issue: Timeline not appearing

Solution: Timeline only appears when events are recorded. Try making larger slider movements.

Issue: Events appear at wrong time positions

Solution: Timeline scales based on recording duration. Longer recordings will compress events toward the left.

Issue: Too many events being recorded

Solution: Threshold filtering should prevent micro-movements. Check smooth DMX settings.
